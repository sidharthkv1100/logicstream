Who We Are

Engineering the Future of Compliance

LogicStream Labs was born out of a deep-rooted passion for electronics, communication, and hands-on hardware prototyping. We are a dedicated team of engineers focused on bridging the critical gap between raw microcontroller architecture and high-level enterprise software.

Our approach to innovation goes beyond just building robust circuits and writing optimized C++ firmware. We fundamentally believe that the future of technology must be built on equitable, inclusive foundations. We design our compliance, security, and attendance systems to ensure that everyone has a seat at the table—creating environments that are as accessible as they are secure.

From legacy assembly logic to modern ESP32 sensor telemetry, we aren't just adapting to the future of physical and digital security. We are engineering it.
